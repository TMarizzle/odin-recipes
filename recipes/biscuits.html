Easy Drop Biscuits

Description

I like to use this recipe for drop biscuits: Biscuits

It's pretty easy! Basically, you need flour, milk, butter, and a few other dry ingredients. Use cold hard butter and shred it with a cheese grater before mixing into the flour. You can drop the biscuit dough straight into the cast iron skillet or cut them up with a circle cookie cutter and place on a baking tray.

Ingredients

  • 240 grams (~2 cups) all-purpose flour
  • 14 grams (1 tablespoon) baking powder
  • 1 3/4 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 113 grams (1 stick or 1/2 cup) unsalted butter, very cold and cut into small pieces
  • 170-227 grams (3/4 to 1 cup) milk or buttermilk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F
  2. In large bowl, whisk together dry ingredients
  3. Add chunks of the cold butter into the dry ingredients and use a pastry cutter or fork to combine
  4. Add milk or buttermilk to mixture and stir until just combined
  5. Drop large mounds of batter into a buttered cast iron skillet or onto a parchment lined baking sheet
  6. Bake for 16-20 minutes until golden brown
  7. Brush with melted butter after biscuits have finished baking

As a bonus, you can mix in shredded cheddar cheese when mixing the dry ingredients and then brush biscuits with a mixture of melted butter and garlic powder. Will taste just like some Red Lobster biscuits!
