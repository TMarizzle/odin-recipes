Pizza Dough

Gotta start with some homemade pizza dough. I like to use this recipe from King Arthur baking: Pizza Dough Recipe

Get to Baking!

  • Gather ingredients
  • Combine dry ingredients in large bowl and mix until combined
  • Activate yeast (if using active dry yeast) by adding a little bit of sugar to warm water and then adding the yeast to the water
  • Let yeast sit for about 5-10 minutes and it should start foaming (if not, your yeast might be dead)
  • Once yeast has started foaming, add to dry ingredients along with some olive oil
  • Mix all ingredients to form sticky dough ball (add more flour if needed)
  • Let dough rise for 15-20 minutes, punch down and let rise again for another 15-20 minutes
  • Move dough into oiled baking pan and slowly spread/stretch dough to fill pan
  • Let rise for 60-90 minutes
  • Add toppings and bake!
